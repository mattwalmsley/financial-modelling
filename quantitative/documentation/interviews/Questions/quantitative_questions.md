- What is the linearity property of expectations?

- What do we mean when we say that two sets are equal?

- How do we define a probability space?
- What is meant by $N!$, i.e. $N$ factorial?
- What is the exponential distribution?

- What is the cumulative distribution function of a continuous random variable?
- What is meant by the conditional expectation of a discrete random variable, given an event?
